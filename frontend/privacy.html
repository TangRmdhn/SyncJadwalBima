Privacy Policy for SyncJadwal

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SyncJadwal ("the Application"). This web application is designed to help students of UPN "Veteran" Yogyakarta synchronize their class schedules from the BIMA academic portal directly to their personal Google Calendar.

2. Information We Access

When you grant access, our Application interacts with your Google Account to use the following Google API Scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar. This permission allows the Application to create new calendar events (your class schedules) on your primary Google Calendar.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your Google user data is used exclusively for creating calendar events based on the schedule data you provide. We do not read, store, share, or sell your data for any other purpose.

4. Compliance with Google Policies

SyncJadwal's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
